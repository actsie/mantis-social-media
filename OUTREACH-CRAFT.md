# Outreach Craft Reference

**Before drafting ANY comment or tweet, read the relevant craft doc:**

| Platform | File | What It Covers |
|----------|------|----------------|
| **X (Twitter)** | `outreach/x/tweet-craft-notes.md` | Building context, personal reflection not directive, banned words |
| **Reddit** | `outreach/reddit/comment-craft-notes.md` | Validate + add one thing, no "you should", banned words |
| **Instagram** | `outreach/instagram/comment-craft-notes.md` | Name something specific, hype is complete, questions about them not us |

---

## **Pre-Post Checklist (All Platforms)**

Before presenting ANY draft as "final":

```
□ Search for dashes: - — → replace with periods/spaces
□ Search for quotes: " ' → rephrase
□ Search for banned words: genuinely, actually, vibe, weird, resonate, etc.
□ Check ending: reflective not directive (no "you should", "worth thinking about if")
□ Read backwards: catch auto-correct misses
□ THEN present as final
```

- ❌ Templates to copy
- ❌ Structures to replicate
- ❌ Excuses to stop thinking

## **What These ARE**

- ✅ Quality calibration
- ✅ Mistake prevention
- ✅ Continuity across sessions
