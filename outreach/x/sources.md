## New Approach (2026-03-06): Explore Page > Cached Tweets

**Cached tweets are deprecated** — they go stale fast (2019 tweets in 2026, etc.).

**New flow for Mode 1/2 posts:**
1. Navigate to https://x.com/explore
2. Scan for relevant posts: salon owners venting, beauty biz struggles, small biz marketing tips
3. **Max 3 snapshots** to find a hook — if nothing, post standalone
4. QRT the post with your take

**Why this works better:**
- ✅ Always fresh content
- ✅ Real ICP voices (salon owners, nail techs, beauty biz owners)
- ✅ No cache maintenance
- ✅ Can react to trending topics in the beauty/salon space

**What to look for:**
- Salon/spa owners talking about booking friction, no-shows, slow months
- Nail techs asking about client acquisition
- Beauty biz marketing accounts posting tips
- Small biz struggles that intersect with our ICP

**Mode 3/4:** No source needed — personal observation posts.

## Posting Flows

### Flow A: Explore Page QRT (Mode 1/2 — preferred)
1. https://x.com/explore → scan for relevant post
2. Max 3 snapshots to find hook
3. Repost → Quote → type your take → Post
4. Log: postType="qrt", quotedTweetUrl, sourceHuntSnapshots

### Flow B: Standalone (Mode 3/4 — no source needed)
1. https://x.com → compose
2. Type tweet → Post
3. Log: postType="standard", sourceHuntSnapshots=0

### Flow C: Source Link Reply (optional credibility boost)
1. Post standalone tweet
2. Reply to own tweet with source URL (BrightLocal, Google, etc.)
3. Log: replyUrl

**Rule:** Max 3 snapshots hunting. If nothing found, post standalone. A clean take beats a forced citation.
