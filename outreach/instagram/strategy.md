# Instagram Strategy — fountainofprofit.com / fix-your-page

## Goal
Build presence as @stacyd0nna in the nail/beauty space, then use that credibility
to DM salon and beauty business owners with a targeted website rebuild offer.

---

## Phase Plan

### Phase 1 — Presence Building (Feb 26 → Feb 28, 3 days)
**What:** 8 comments/day on nail/beauty posts via hashtags
**Target accounts:** Active nail salons, beauty creators, nail brands
**Tone:** Genuine, specific, community-first (see tone-guide.md)
**Goal:** Build real comment history, look like a real person in the space
**No DMs yet** — account needs history first or DMs go straight to spam

### Phase 2 — Targeted DM Outreach (starts ~March 1)
**What:** DM US-based beauty salons and nail salons with the fix-your-page offer
**Who to DM:**
- Nail salons with a link in bio that goes to a weak/broken/generic website
- Salons with lots of posts but no clear booking path in their bio
- Beauty salons where the "book now" button is missing or goes to a dead page
- Med spas, lash studios, brow bars — any beauty service business

**How to find them:**
- Browse hashtags: #nailsalon #nailsalonowner #beautysalon #salonowner #nailtech
- Filter for US accounts (check location tags, city hashtags like #nailsalonnyc)
- Look for recent posts (active accounts only)
- Check their link in bio before DMing — only DM if there's a real website problem to point to

**DM Template (adapt from X template):**
> hey this is a random dm but I looked at your site and I think [SPECIFIC ISSUE] may be making new clients work a little harder than they should before booking. We'd love to do a 1 day landing page remake for [SALON NAME]! Here's a quick sample of what we mean: https://www.fountainofprofit.com/sample/beauty-salon. If you're curious, reply yes and I'll send details. If not, no worries at all.

**Volume:** 5-10 DMs/day max — IG limits outreach on new-ish accounts
**Track all DMs** in `outreach/tracker.csv`

### Phase 3 — Follow-up & Convert (ongoing)
- Follow up once on DMs that were opened but not replied to (wait 3-4 days)
- Track reply rate and refine the hook
- Build a shortlist of "warm" accounts (liked our posts, replied to comments)

---

## What Makes a Good DM Target

**Green flags:**
- Has a website but it's clearly outdated, generic, or hard to navigate
- Posts regularly (active account = active business)
- Has engagement (real clients commenting)
- Link in bio goes to a Wix/Weebly/generic page with no clear booking
- Bio says "book via DM" or "call to book" (= no real booking page)

**Red flags:**
- Already has a great website (don't waste the DM)
- Very large account (they have web teams)
- No posts in 3+ months (not active)
- Already using a strong booking platform with a clean landing page
