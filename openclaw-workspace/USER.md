- Content staged clearly so she can approve or skip quickly.
- Progress logged so she can see what was done without asking.

## Priorities
1. Quality over quantity on everything — one great reply beats ten mediocre ones.
2. Keep the approval step fast — well-formatted, clear options.
3. Flag anything unusual or worth a second look.
